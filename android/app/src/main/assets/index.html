WAVE SHAPER — OSC 1 DRAG TO SCULPT
This IS your sound, drawn as a wave. Smooth curves = soft and warm. Sharp jagged edges = harsh and buzzy. Drag to redraw it. The green scope below shows what is actually coming out of the speakers right now.
STOCK WAVE — SINE
OSC 1
The main voice. SAW = bright buzz · SQR = hollow video-game · SIN = pure whistle · TRI = soft flute. OCTAVE/SEMI move it up or down in pitch, LEVEL is its loudness.
OSC 2
A second voice stacked on the first for thickness. Set SEMI to +7 or +12 for instant fatness. DETUNE a little for a wide chorus shimmer.
FILTER
The tone knob. CUTOFF low = muffled and underwater · high = bright and sharp. RESONANCE adds a whistle right at the cutoff point. LOWPASS keeps lows, HIGHPASS keeps highs.
ENVELOPE (ADSR)
How the note moves over time: ATTACK = fade-in speed (0 = instant hit) · DECAY = fall after the peak · SUSTAIN = loudness while held · RELEASE = fade-out after you let go.
LFO
An invisible hand that slowly wiggles a control for you. RATE = wiggle speed · DEPTH = wiggle size. Aim it at FILTER for wah-wah, PITCH for siren wobble, AMPLITUDE for tremolo.

SKRiMPAD HELP
PADS Tap to play. Right-click: rename, import MP3 sample, pattern presets.
SEQ Click cells to program. ▶ runs pads + key seq together.
KEYS Play a key, click KEY SEQ steps to place it. Right-click a step = sustain. Right-click keyboard = import pitched sample.
SYNTH Drag the wave to shape the sound. ◀ ▶ picks OSC 1 / OSC 2.
FX Toggle pedals; graphics show the wave effect.
LOOPS R = drum looper, K = keys looper. ● record, ▶ play, ✕ clear. 📥 import backing track · 📤 export audio.
AI 🔍 detects your key · 🥁 writes a style drum pattern · 🎼 arranges your key-seq notes into a melody.
🖌 Customize: skins and tile drag-to-rearrange.
LANES L1–L5 above the key seq = 5 melody lanes, each with its own sound bank. Right-click (or hold) a lane to mute. ▶ KEYS plays lanes without drums.
KEYS SHORTCUTS SPACE = play/pause · SHIFT+SPACE = keys only · ← → = BPM (SHIFT = ±5) · ↑ ↓ = volume · 1–0 = pads 1–10 · SHIFT+1–0 = pads 11–20.
PIANO KEYS A S D F G H J K = white keys · W E T Y U = black keys · Z X = octave down/up · C = sustain toggle.
TOUCH On phone/tablet: HOLD a pad, lane, fader or the keyboard to open its edit menu (same as right-click).
AI SEARCH Type what you want in the AI tab ("jungle beat at 174", "make a laser") — or tap 🤖 in the banner for the full AI interface on desktop.
🛑 PANIC Kills every sound instantly — loops, held notes, echo tails, backing tracks. Also on the ESC key.
✂ EDITOR Splice and snip any sample: ✂ on library sounds, or right-click a pad → EDIT SAMPLE. Drag to select, SNIP keeps it, CUT removes it.
🕐 60s Retro-record: the app always remembers the last 60 seconds of output. Played something brilliant without recording? Tap 🕐 60s in LOOP to rescue it.
🎚 REMIX Splits any song file into bass / vocals / drums stems (approximate EQ + mid-side split) and puts them on pads 13/15/16.
LIB TAB Your imported, generated and spliced sounds are saved on this device and come back after restart.
📁 FILE
Saves your whole session — patterns, key lanes, pad names, mixer, synth and FX settings — on this device. EXPORT downloads it as a file you can share. (Imported audio samples are not saved — reload those.)
